What the timeline shows

Peak detections cluster in late spring and early summer. Differences between confidence categories reflect detection thresholds and environmental conditions.

Spatial context

Detections concentrate in historically high-risk regions. Local weather and fuel load influence the apparent density.

Interpreting confidence

Confidence categories reflect sensor thresholds and algorithms. Proportions help compare periods with very different totals.

Within-month variability

Day-to-day volatility often corresponds with heat, wind, and lightning events. Clusters indicate persistent conditions.
